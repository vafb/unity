Custom Motorcycle Coverage for Customized Rides

Your bike fits your personality and style—so should your motorcycle insurance. Your local agent can provide a free quote for a range of options with varying deductibles, premiums, and coverages, then help sort through the right one for you.

Liability Coverage

This basic motorcycle insurance pays out when your bike causes damage to someone or something else. If you drive an older motorcycle and don’t have many other assets to protect, you may not need much more coverage than this. Check with your local agent to find out.

Collision Coverage

This picks up where liability insurance leaves off—it covers your own motorcycle when you cause an accident or hit a stationary object. This coverage is especially important if you ride a newer model or carry a loan.

Other Than Collision Coverage

Sometimes called comprehensive coverage, this option covers damage to your bike not caused by a collision. It takes care of issues like fire, theft, vandalism, glass breakage, and weather-related damage.

Medical Expense Benefit

This can offset your health insurance deductible and pay out for your passengers’ injuries if necessary. It’s also an added buffer if you’re liable in an accident that causes serious injuries.

Towing & Labor

Bikes break down at the most inconvenient times. This package includes towing, gas delivery, tire replacement, and more to get you back on the road as quickly as possible, no matter where or when you need help.

Uninsured Motorist Coverage

If someone else causes a crash and doesn’t have insurance, or if their insurance isn’t enough to repair or replace the damage they caused to you, this coverage kicks in.

Income Loss Benefits

Motorcycle accidents can cause serious, life-altering injuries that may force you to miss time at work. This coverage can help provide for your family if accident-related injuries keep you off the job and cause a loss of income.

Other Coverage

Talking with your local agent is the best way to find out what riders or specific coverage are recommended for your specific situation.
