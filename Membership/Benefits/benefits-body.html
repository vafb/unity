Becoming a Virginia Farm Bureau member is a simple yet powerful way to put your support squarely behind the farms that produce your family’s food and other products. When you join Virginia Farm Bureau, your $40 annual membership supports programs, educational tools and events that help agriculture prosper in Virginia, and you get discounts on travel, hotels, car rentals and much more.

Member Advantages Built for Virginia's Farmers

Products & Equipment

For more than 40 years, the Virginia Farm Bureau has provided members with a variety of quality supplies, from the Farm Bureau warehouse and more than 250 Farm Bureau-approved dealers statewide.

Marketplace

Use Farm Bureau’s online Marketplace and member magazine classified ads to find and sell farm products and services.

Grassroots

When you join Virginia Farm Bureau, you support a wide variety of programs, educational tools and events—in your own community—that help agriculture prosper.

Producer Advantage

Members who farm can take advantage of Farm Bureau's commodity marketing and business development services.

Safety Advantage

Members can save on slow-moving vehicle emblems and other safety equipment and can receive fire extinguishers for eligible farm equipment listed on a Virginia Farm Bureau insurance policy.
