Marketplace

Members can use Farm Bureau’s online Marketplace to find and sell farm products and to find and share information about agricultural events. Your membership also entitles you to one free 15-word classified ad per year in one of Farm Bureau’s member magazines.

Farmer's Markets

Find fresh, locally grown foods available from Virginia Farm Bureau producer members near you—at on-farm markets and through subscription-system community-supported agriculture operations, or CSAs.

Classified Ads

Virginia Farm Bureau members may place 45-word Marketplace listings. Listings submitted here, online, are free. Members also receive one free 15-word listing in Farm Bureau News or Cultivate magazine per membership per year.
