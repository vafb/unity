Commodity Market Information

Our staff serves as a voice and a resource for producers at the local, state, and national levels. From aquaculture to wine grapes, VFB has advisory committees dedicated to interacting with the private sector, regulatory agencies, Virginia Cooperative Extension and a wide range of organizations on behalf of producer members.

Grain Marketing

Our grain division is a licensed grain dealer helping producer members market corn, soybeans, wheat, barley, milo, and oats throughout our region. We offer risk management tools in the form of forward pricing using futures and options on the Chicago Board of Trade. We can arrange hauling, members can buy and sell options, hedge with futures, and get updated market information and news.

Business Development

Through partnership with VA FAIRS, producer members can explore or expand their value-added agricultural business. VA FAIRS offers business development services, print and online resources, and in-person consultations to interested members.

Farm Products & Equipment

Farm Bureau also is a competitive supplier of truck and farm machinery tires, batteries, and oil and grease products, as well as baler twine, disk blades, plow parts and cutting parts for hay equipment and combines—from its warehouse in Henrico County and through 250 approved dealers statewide.
