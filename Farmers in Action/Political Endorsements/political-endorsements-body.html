Political endorsements are based on the recommendations of local committees of farmers. Candidates who receive VFBF AgPAC endorsements have demonstrated a clear understanding of the needs and challenges farmers are facing and/or have proven their support through their favorable voting records while holding positions in the General Assembly.

Who Does Virginia Farm Bureau Endorse?

Virginia Farm Bureau Federation AgPAC, the political action committee of Virginia Farm Bureau Federation will be evaluating the candidates for the Governor, Lt. Governor, Attorney General and House of Delegates in July and August 2017. Our process is a grassroots process whereby local farmers are appointed at the local level and elected at the state level to interview and/or evaluate candidates running for these offices. These endorsements will be released late August/early September.
