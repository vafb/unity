Virginia Farm Bureau supports farmers through a number of programs to create an environment where agriculture can prosper in order to improve the lives of Virginians. Farmers are facing many challenges: a changing legislative and regulatory environment; a need for business networking opportunities; a need to improve leadership skills; a need to educate others about farmers and farming.

How we support Virginia's farmers

Legislative Activites

We are the voice of Virginia agriculture in the General Assembly. Learn more about our legislative activities representing our farmer members.

Political Endorsements

Virginia Farm Bureau Federation AgPAC is the political action committee of the organization. Learn more about the state office candidates who best support agriculture, forestry and Farm Bureau issues.

Young Farmers

Business networking, leadership skills, professional and personal development are some of the advantages to being involved in the Young Farmers activities.

Women's Program

Learn how to help others learn about agriculture and farming in your local community as well as being involved in the community outreach and political activities of the organization.
